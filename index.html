Research Interest: My research interests are in geometric analysis, focusing on geometric inequalities and geometric objects under curvature bounds.
Some tools I use include optimal transport, stratification of singular sets, and calculus of variation.
A side interest of mine is about approximation problems in reproducing kernel Hilbert spaces (RKHS).
